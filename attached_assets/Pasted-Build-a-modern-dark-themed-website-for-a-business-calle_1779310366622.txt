Build a modern dark-themed website for a business called “Scale Surge”.

Scale Surge helps home service businesses increase revenue through automation systems, AI-powered lead response, and business process improvements. The first and primary service is “Missed Call Text Back” — when a customer misses a call, they instantly receive an automated text response so leads are not lost.

The website should focus on business outcomes, not technical jargon. Avoid making the site feel like a generic AI startup. The messaging should focus on:

recovering lost leads
increasing booked jobs
faster customer response times
reducing missed opportunities
helping trades businesses grow revenue

Target audience:

Home service businesses
Trades companies
Local service businesses
Examples include plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, HVAC companies, builders, cleaners, etc.
